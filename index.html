Steven Millard Elementary is a public school located in Fremont, CA, which is in a large suburb setting. The student population of Steven Millard Elementary is 528 and the school serves K-6. At Steven Millard Elementary, 69% of students scored at or above the proficient level for math, and 71% scored at or above that level for reading.

Millard Elementary's Lalith (5th-grade) and Elizabeth (6th-grade) were named finalists in the school's 2018 Spelling Bee. They will represent the Mustangs at the District Spelling Bee January 29th at Glenmoor Elementary School.
